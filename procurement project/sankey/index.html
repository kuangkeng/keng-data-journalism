Where the Top 100 received their contracts?



Five ministries contributed to nearly 70% of the contract value received by the top 100 companies.


These ministries are:
  • Health Ministry 22.29%
  • Prime Minister's Department 13.94%
  • Ministry of Rural and Regional Development 12.73%
  • Works Ministry 11.42%
  • Defense Ministry 8.71%


This chart shows where the contracts were from. Each line represents a contract and the thickness represents the value of the contract. The number next to each ministry label is the percentage of total contract value given to the 100 companies.
