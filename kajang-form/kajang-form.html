• Keep Kajang Polls Clean and Fair

  • Elections are an important part of our democracy and citizens of this country have a duty to make sure that competing candidates and their agents adhere to the election rules.

    If you have witnessed violations of election laws during the Kajang by-election, we want to know about it.

    Regardless of whether you are a voter or not, you can inform us about suspected electoral irregularities during the campaign or on polling day by filling in the form below.

    Malaysiakini will then compile the list in the Keep Kajang Polls Clean and Fair webpage on a daily basis.

    Confidentiality of personal details is assured.

    Please limit your submission to ONE incident only. Multiple submissions are permitted.
